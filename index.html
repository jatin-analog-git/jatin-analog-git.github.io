I am an Analog IC Design Engineer specializing in high-performance integrated circuits. I leverage Cadence Virtuoso, Spectre, HSPICE, and Mentor Calibre to size transistors, close layout parasitic extraction (PEX) loops, and validate sub-micron CMOS designs across PVT process corners. My background spans early MATLAB architectural modeling to tape-out ready silicon, with a particular focus on low-power data converters and transconductance amplifiers.
